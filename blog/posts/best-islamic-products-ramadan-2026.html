Tips for a Spiritually Productive Ramadan

Beyond physical products, here are a few practical tips to make the most of Ramadan 2026:

  • Set a Quran goal — aim to complete one Juz (chapter) per day to finish the entire Quran during Ramadan.
  • Prepare your prayer space early — dedicate a quiet corner with your prayer mat and a Quran so it’s always ready.
  • Stock up on Sunnah foods — dates, honey, olive oil, and black seed are all recommended in Islamic tradition.
  • Calculate and pay your Zakat early — many scholars recommend paying Zakat before Ramadan so you can focus fully on worship during the month.
  • Give Sadaqah daily — even a small daily charity multiplies in reward during Ramadan.
