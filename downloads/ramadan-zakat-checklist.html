Ramadan Zakat Checklist 2026

Your complete step-by-step guide to calculating and paying Zakat this Ramadan.

Step 1: Preparation (1-2 Weeks Before Ramadan)

  • Set your Zakat anniversary date (hawl). If unsure, use the 1st of Ramadan (~February 19, 2026).
  • Gather all bank statements (checking, savings, fixed deposits).
  • List all gold and silver items with approximate weight in grams.
  • Check current market value of investments (stocks, mutual funds, crypto).
  • Note any business inventory held for sale.
  • List outstanding debts you owe (credit cards, loans due, bills).

Step 5: Pay Your Zakat

  • Choose a reputable Islamic charity (see our Best Islamic Charities 2026 guide).
  • Ensure you select the "Zakat" fund (not general donations).
  • Pay your Zakat al-Mal amount calculated above.
  • Keep a receipt for your tax records (Zakat is tax-deductible in many countries).

Final Review

  • All gold and silver included in calculation (including personal jewelry).
  • All bank accounts and investments accounted for.
  • Current gold price used for Nisab (not outdated values).
  • Zakat al-Mal paid to eligible recipients or charity.
  • Fitrana paid for every household member before Eid prayer.
  • Made du'a for acceptance and blessings on your wealth.

Ramadan 2026 Key Dates

  • ~February 19: Ramadan began (confirmed by moon sighting)
  • ~March 20: Last 10 nights begin
  • ~March 24-28: Laylat al-Qadr (odd nights)
  • ~March 29: Last day of Ramadan — Fitrana deadline
  • ~March 30: Eid al-Fitr — Pay Fitrana before prayer
