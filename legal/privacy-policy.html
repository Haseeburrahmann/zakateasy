Privacy Policy

How we handle your data

Last Updated: February 8, 2026

Overview

ZakatEasy ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our website at zakateasy.netlify.app.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you use the Zakat calculator, you enter financial information such as asset values and liabilities. This information is processed entirely in your web browser and is never transmitted to or stored on our servers. We do not have access to any financial data you enter into the calculator.

Automatically Collected Information

We may collect certain information automatically when you visit our website, including:

  • Usage data: Pages visited, time spent on site, referral source (via Google Analytics)
  • Device information: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution
  • IP address: Used for approximate geographic location (country/region level) for analytics purposes

Local Storage

We use your browser's localStorage to cache gold and silver price data for up to 24 hours. This data is stored on your device only and is not transmitted to us. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage.

How We Use Information

We use automatically collected information to:

  • Understand how visitors use our website (analytics)
  • Improve our calculator and content
  • Monitor website performance and fix issues
  • Display relevant advertisements (if applicable)

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand website traffic and usage patterns. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous usage data. You can opt out by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Google AdSense

We may display advertisements through Google AdSense. Google may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ad Settings.

Metals Price API

We fetch gold and silver prices from a third-party API service. Your IP address may be visible to this service during the API request. No personal information is transmitted.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies for:

  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics tracking (can be opted out)
  • Advertising cookies: Google AdSense personalization (can be opted out)
  • Essential cookies: None currently used

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies will not affect the core functionality of the Zakat calculator.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the limited data we collect. Since financial calculations are performed entirely in your browser, your sensitive financial data never leaves your device.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of Google Analytics tracking
  • Opt out of personalized advertisements
  • Clear localStorage data from your browser
  • Disable cookies in your browser settings
  • Request information about what data we hold (we hold no personal data)

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: support@zakateasy.netlify.app
